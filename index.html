The director's words

Close to two decades back, I started my professional journey as a part of a Large Organisation doing something brand new (we didn't use the word "Startup" those days). This became a theme of all the jobs that I was a part of, across Financial Services, Infrastructure and Hospitality industries. With the confidence and learning these Giants equipped me with, I started Winning Edge on my Dinning table. We grew from my Dinning Table to bigger offices and more friends joined this journey. All Services

Our Practice Areas

Search Solutions

Executive Search firm for assisting clients with “Critical Positions”. At Search Solutions, our solutions start at a preemptive stage ( Talent Mapping ) and further into both Retained Executive Search and Contingency Recruitment Solutions. We have successfully assisted with 100+ C suite searches, worked across 145+ geographies and 50+ organisations. We through innovative methodologies, have acted as catalyst to organisational learning , effective recruitment and staffing.

HI3

HI3 is a disruption product that had been designed to offer our partners get a customised recruitment process that gives them the advantage of both flexibility and pricing. HI3 addresses the six key challenges facing the HR team for any organisation.Our hiring solutions start with talent mapping and trend analysis followed by hiring and getting tied up with retention/ coaching at 3 months. The HI3 product , gives you the option to pick and choose any part of the product.

Impact Your Tomorrow
Our workshops look at learning as a solution to Business, Behaviour, Leadership and Team Challenges. With a team which comes with a plethora of Industry (Conventional and Services) experience, we provide solutions which create Impact and deliver results. With Coaching as the basic ethos behind each intervention , we believe that the client has most the answers. These workshops work on creating a learning environment and facilitating the same.
Learning Lunches

In current time of Time Poverty, Winning Edge provides you with a Bite Size Learning Solution. This a 60-75minute Corporate Training Session, with singular focus on delivering a session for 6-8 executives, who share a common challenge at their office. The time bound crispness of these 75-90minute interventions are a response to, specific solutions to subtle but critical business needs. The biggest benefit is, the focus on Outcomes rather than on Coverage.

RESUMAKE

A Design HR Solution for busy executives, we at ‘Resumake’ assist you in creating a customised resume' (CV), and updating your online professional presence through a focussed discussion with you to bring out and represent your strengths for you get a shot at your desired career move. Through this service, we help you showcase your strengths and project you in the best way across platforms, for you take a shot at your dream job.
