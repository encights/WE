Founder and CEO of the Winning Edge

Leadership and Career Coach | Headhunter | Corporate Trainer | Co Author - Resumake

HR professional, armchair philosopher, activist for active good, fellow life-traveller, father, son, husband, brother and friend are some of the ways(not necessarily in the same order) that I am recognised as.

Close to two decades back, I started my professional journey as a part of a Large Organisation doing something brand new (we didn't use the word "Startup" those days).

This became a theme of all the jobs that I was a part of, across Financial Services, Infrastructure and Hospitality industries. With the confidence and learning these Giants equipped me with, I started Winning Edge on my Dinning table in September 2005.

We grew from my Dinning Table to bigger offices and more friends joined this journey. What started as recruitment firm in 2005 focussed on providing, top talent to leading organisations, evolved into a firm "Focussed on the Human in Human Resources".

This lead to sub-organisations specializing in Executive Search(Search Solutions) , Learning & Development(Impact Your Tomorrow | Learning Lunches) , Executive Coaching and HR Content & Design Practices (Resumake).

Our Search Solutions team has successfully assisted with 100+ C suite searches, worked across 145+ geographies and 50+ organisations. We through innovative methodologies, have acted as catalyst to organisational learning , effective recruitment and staffing.

The Learning & Development wing has advised, assisted and coached some of the top Global Organisations, helping them develop leaders at all levels.

Resumake is a Design HR Solution for busy executives, where we assist them in creating a customised resume' (CV), updating their online professional presence through a focussed discussion with them to bring out and represent their strengths for them to get a shot at their desired career move.
